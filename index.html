The Black Panther

The black panther is the name given to jaguars or leopards that have a type of melanism, in which their standard golden and spotted fur is coated in all black.This is considered one of the largest predators though it is one o fthe smaller large cats. Of the large cats, however, this animal has the strongest bite force per pound.

These animals typically feast on any smaller animal. With their incredible bite force and thier extreme agility and jumping ability, they suceed as actiuve hunters consistantly.
